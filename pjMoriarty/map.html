The aim of the wave9 mobile application is to set the solar model within a recognizable context, representing the vast distances of planets as elements of the user environment.

With the overlay of augmented reality renderings, the underlying nature of these planetary relationships is made accessible through the viewer context, injecting meaning into the physical environment in a mode of interspatial poetics.
