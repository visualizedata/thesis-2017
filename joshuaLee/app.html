Plato once said, "You can discover more about a person in an hour of play than in a year of conversation." 44% of the internet population plays some form of an online game, and in real terms that is about 700 million people across the world. What do people do in online games? Who do they meet and who do they talk to? What motivates them to become so invested in these shared virtual environments? This visualization investigates player interactions in the massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) Aion. Three players were randomly assigned to have their gameplay interactions visualized through three critical lenses: in-game money transactions, private messages, and skills and damage.
